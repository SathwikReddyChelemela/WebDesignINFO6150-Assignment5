Sathwik Says:

"Hello, I'm Sathwik Reddy, a passionate fashion designer from Hyderabad, Telangana. I've always been deeply connected to the rich cultural heritage of my hometown, which has greatly influenced my approach to fashion. Beyond my work in the fashion industry, I've taken on the role of a mentor, guiding aspiring designers, and I've had the opportunity to share my insights on fashion and design through lectures at esteemed institutions.My design philosophy revolves around the principles of impeccable tailoring, intricate detailing, and an unwavering commitment to using the finest fabrics. I strive to blend classical design techniques with modern, avant-garde elements, resulting in garments that are both timeless and fashion-forward. I remain dedicated to the art of couture and will continue to push the boundaries of fashion design, leaving a lasting impact on the industry. Thank you for your interest in my work and my journey as a fashion designer."
